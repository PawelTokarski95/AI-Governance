## Overview

This repository contains a collection of projects focused on AI Governance, AI Risk Management, Model Risk Management, and Responsible AI practices. The goal of this portfolio is to demonstrate practical understanding of how machine learning systems should be designed, evaluated, and monitored in regulated environments such as banking, fintech, and insurance.

## Methodology

The projects are based on a combination of:

- Industry frameworks like NIST AI Risk Management Framework, ISO/IEC standards
- Machine learning model evaluation techniques
- Best practices from AI governance literature
- Regulatory guidelines for high-risk AI systems

## Purpose of this Portfolio

This repository is designed to demonstrate practical understanding of:

- How AI systems fail in real-world environments
- How to assess and mitigate model and data risks
- How to document AI systems for regulatory and audit purposes
- How to align machine learning systems with governance frameworks


## Disclaimer

Some projects are conceptual and based on simulated scenarios. They are intended for educational and portfolio purposes, not production use.
